
#### Flags

At the Remembrance Day ceremony in Charlottetown on November 11, 2016, there were eleven flag-bearers. For years now, I have wanted to enumerate these flags, as some are not familiar (to me).

As a small tribute to men and women who serve our country, this is a celebration of these flags. (If there is an error, please email codetojoy [@t] gmail com)
